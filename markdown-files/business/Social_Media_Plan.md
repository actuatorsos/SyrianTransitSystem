# Damascus Transit Technologies — Social Media Launch Plan

> **Channels:** Twitter/X · LinkedIn · Facebook
> **Duration:** 2-week launch campaign
> **Languages:** Arabic (primary) + English
> **Narrative theme:** Syrian innovation · Reconstruction through technology · Real benefits for daily commuters

---

## STRATEGY

### Objectives
1. Establish DAM as Syria's authoritative voice on public transit technology
2. Build awareness among potential government partners, donors, and operators
3. Generate passenger excitement about the platform ahead of pilot launch

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional** but human — we are solving a real problem real people face every day
- **Optimistic** — this is a story of Syrian innovation and reconstruction
- **Specific** — cite real numbers (45-min wait, 2.5M commuters, 8 routes) not vague claims
- **Bilingual** — Arabic posts lead; English translation follows or appears as separate post

### Hashtag Strategy
**Arabic:** #دمشق_ترانزيت · #مواصلات_دمشق · #سوريا_تتقدم · #ذكاء_المدينة
**English:** #DamascusTransit · #SmartTransit · #SyriaRebuild · #OpenSource · #MENA

### Day 3 — The Technology: "How It Works"

**Twitter/X (English):**
> Here's how the DAM platform works — in 5 steps:
> 1️⃣ A $30 GPS device is installed on each vehicle
> 2️⃣ It sends live positions every 5 seconds via 4G
> 3️⃣ Operators see their full fleet on a live map
> 4️⃣ Passengers get live ETAs on a free mobile app
