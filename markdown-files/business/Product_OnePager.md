### The Problem

Every day, **2.5 million Damascenes** navigate a public transit network with zero digital infrastructure. No GPS tracking. No schedules. No passenger information. The average wait at a bus stop is **45 minutes**. Operators manage fleets on paper. The Ministry of Transport has no data.

---

### Our Solution

The **DAM Platform** is Syria's first real-time GPS fleet management and passenger information system — purpose-built for Damascus.

| For Operators | For Passengers | For Government |
|---------------|----------------|----------------|
| Live fleet map, updated every 5 seconds | Live ETAs on a free mobile app | Route analytics, ridership data |
| Driver accountability & trip logging | Arabic + English interface | GTFS-ready export for Google Maps |
| Geofence alerts & dispatch tools | No app download required (PWA) | First-ever network performance dashboard |
